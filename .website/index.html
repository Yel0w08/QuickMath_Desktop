What's in it

⚡

Fully offline

No internet, no account, no telemetry. Just double-click and play. Your saves stay on your machine.

🎯

5 difficulty levels

From easy++ (1–10) to insane (100–1000). Hard and insane are locked until you earn enough coins.

⭐

XP and coins

Every correct answer gives XP and coins. Spend coins in the shop, level up as you practice.

📊

Stats page

See how many problems you've solved, broken down by operation. Simple and honest tracking.

🛒

In-app shop

Unlock harder difficulties and cosmetic items. No pay to win — everything costs coins you earn.

🔓

Open source

Built with C# and WinForms. The whole thing is on GitHub if you want to dig in or contribute.

Give it a try

Grab the latest release from GitHub. Unzip, run the exe — no installer, no .NET to install separately, nothing extra.
